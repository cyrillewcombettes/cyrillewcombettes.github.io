Bio

I am a quantitative researcher at Capital Fund Management in Paris. Prior to this, I was a postdoc working with Jérôme Bolte. I obtained a Ph.D. in Machine Learning from Georgia Tech in May 2021, where I worked on developing efficient algorithms for constrained optimization.
