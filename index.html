About me

I am a second-year Ph.D. candidate in Machine Learning at Georgia Tech, advised by Sebastian Pokutta. My research interests lie at the intersection of optimization and machine learning. I am currently working on designing fast and sparse methods in convex optimization.

I received a M.Sc. in Applied Mathematics from École Centrale Paris and a Master of Business Analytics from MIT. Prior to joining Georgia Tech, I have worked on multiple real-world analytics projects, including as a Data Scientist Intern at BCG GAMMA and as a Research Assistant at MIT's Operations Research Center.
